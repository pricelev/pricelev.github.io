Voidwalker Classes

Attunement of Chaos

In the attumenent of Chasos the warlock can hold its grenade to power up its damage. By using its melee ability the warlock can refund grenade energy and heal. Void abilites can cause the enemies to explode. The super fires a slow but large nova bomb that shatters into small nova to deal massive damage. An interesting ability to this super is that it can be shot to shatter faster.

Attunement of Fission

The attunement of fission turs the warlocks grenade into a hand held supernova which can be deadly for players at close range. When getting void ability kills the warlockcan can regin health and ability energy. The super takes away the nova bomb but allows the player to warp around the map and cause mini novas to kill enemies. This super while good in PVE is highly effective in PVP because of movement abilities.

Attunement of Hunger

The attument of hunger allows the warlock to become a self efficient tank. By consuming their grenade or using thier melee ability the warlock gets an ability called devour. Devour heals the warlock on any type of void kill. Devour also gives ability recharge rate. Devour can be held up infinitely given a lot of enemies. Its super shoots a fast traveling novabomb that sits on the enemy rather than exploding.

Stormcaller Classes

Attunement of Conduction

The attunement of conduction makes the warlock do lots of area of affect abilities. Its melee and grenade cause an arc chain that spreads through group of enemies. Damaging enemies with this chain causes ability recharge. The super gives the player the ability to teleport during storm caller.

Attunement of Control

The attunement of control turns the melee charge into a projectile that explodes after a short distance. Defeating enemies cause ionic traces to form that give the warlock reload speed and movement speed. When at low health they warlock increases its movement speed for himself and allies. Instead of the normal arc super the warlock creates a beam of electricity during super.

Attunement of the Elements

In attunement of the elements the warlock provides itself and allies with arc turrets. This can be accomplished through the warlocks rift. During super the warlock emmits a lightning strike at the beginning for extra damage. The super can be extended by killing enemies. The warlock melee ability gives ability energy on use.

Dawnblade Classes

Attunement of Sky

The attunement of sky allows the warlock to have high mobility for PVP. It gains an in air dash and allows the player to float by consuming their grenade. This makes the subclass lethal because most classes have bad in air conditions. The attunement of sky gives accuracy and range buffs for in air combat. The super allows you to through solar blades at the enemy.

Attundment of Grace

The attunment of grace is the most supportive class in the game. It allows the warlock to turn his grenade into a healing effect that allies can run though. This give them a overshield to help with combat. When allies use this grenade spot or you right the warlock recieve blessing of dawn where it greatly improves ability cooldowns. The super creates a well that gives healing and damage amplification for allies. This tends to be helpful in high level content.

Attunement of Flame

The attunement of flame is used to burn enemies. The super has auto tracking to help get kills faster. Upon getting these kills they duration of the super is extended. An interesting part of this subclass kit add pheonix dive to the mix. This allows the warlock to have some in air mobility to heal in a pinch.

Stasis Class

Shadebinder

The shadebinder like all the other stasis subclasses was added more recently. It has some strong abilities mainly with its melee. Its melee turns into a projectile that can instant freeze anything it hits. They also have an adaptation were the rift can freeze enemies on use. The super is the only way the warlock can shatter enemies. Its main attack fires bolts that freeze its targets. The alternate fire sends our a way and shatters any frozen enemy.
