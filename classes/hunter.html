Nightstalker Classes

Way of the Trapper

The way of the trapper uses their super to tether enemies for a long period of time and from a greater range. Their melee charge changes to a smoke bomb that can disorient the enemy. The trapper also can use its dodge to become invisible till they use and ability or weapon or the time runs out. This can be extended by some exotics.

Way of the Wraith

The way of the wraith turns the hunters tethers into blades that the hunter can use around the map. They have the ability to become invisible during super. While at full health the hunter can also gain invisiblity on precision kills and increase melee damage. This super is mainly used in PVP due to its invisiblity uptime.

Way of the Pathfinder

The way of the pathfinder allows the hunter to fire multiple tethers during its super. The trade off is that the tethers are not as strong. This super generates orbs based of the number of enemies tethered. Its melee charge turns into a smoke bomb that can make the hunter and allies nearby invisible for a short perior of time. This subclass is used mostly for PVE content.

Arc Strider Classes

Way of the Warrior

Way of the warrior has abilites that play between your dodge and melee ability. They tend to decrease cooldown on each otehrs user and also increase damage of melees. During super, after performing a dodge the next melee attatack will create a arc damage field on the ground. The Way of the Warrior excells in PVE activity when used with some hunter exotics.

Way of the Current

The way of the Current focuses on melee for the hunter. Instead of the normal melee the hunter performs a strong upper cut to damage the enemy. What makes this path unique is that its super can be used to reflect bullets. This path doesnt really find a home in the current game due to some of the deficiencies of the kit.

Way of the Wind

The way of the Wind is by far the arcstriders strongest kit for dealing with PVP content. It allows the user to not take damage while dodging ini super. Its smelee also disorients their toarget and gets a generious time buff to do this again. The time buff of this is basicly infinite given enough enemies.

Gunslinger Classes

Way of the Outlaw

The way of the Outlaw is the prefered super for gunglinger in pvp. It allows the player to have 6 shots of golden gun instead of three. In returnt he super last a bit shorter than with three. Getting weapon kills allows for reload speed. The hunter melee knife in the also becomes explosive.

Way of a Thousand Cuts

The way of a thousand cuts is a new version of the super that exchanges the gulden gun to a fan of knives. These knives explode on contact. This super is highly effective in PVP when faces lots of enemies. This paths super and melee cause burn which helps with dodge cooldown.

Way of the Sharpshooter

The way of the sharpshooter is intended for PVE content. It gives bonus precision damge for critical hits while also creatign orbs of light on super critical hits. It uses a weighted knife that when getting a precision hit will refund its charge.

Stasis Class

Revenant

The revenant is new to Destiny. It features their idea of cosmic ice which freezes enemies. The hunters melee ability has two charges that are thrown. They can slow enemies that it hits while also bouncing off walls. The dodge can slow enemies near the player. The super throws the hunters hatches and creates a vortex that rotates around an area. The hunter also gets abilites such as shatter dive where the player can dive froxen enemies to shatter them. This causes more damage to enemies in the area.
