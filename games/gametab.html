Gambit

Vendor Info

The vendor for gambit is the Drifter. He is a ancient wielder of light that seems to be a rogue agent for the universe. He provides a lot of the story content for the current seasons. He provides palyers with ingame and weekly bounties that revolve around the gambit gametype.

How to Play

The goal of gambit is to fill your teams bank and kill the primevil that is produced. Players can gather motes by killing enemies on a map that spawn in different ways. Every 25 motes a team deposits they can invade the other team and can cause the lose of motes. After 100 motes are banked the boss is summoned. The game is over when this boss is finished.

Rewards

After each game the player is given five random rolls of the gambit loot pool. Some of the more notable drops are the hand cannon: Bottom Dollar. The armor for gambit are in the form of ornaments that he player can turn on and off.

Iron Banner

Vendor Info

THe vendor for Iron Banner is Lord Salidin. Saladin is a side character for most of the story of destiny. He helps maintain the iron banner event. He provides the rewards and bounties for the event. Unlike most vendors he only has weekly bounties for the players.

Game Play

The gameplay in iron banner is always the same. Its a play on the control gametype from the original pvp version. The twist is that if a team captures all three points on the map, there is a lockdown period where that team is rewarded extra points on kills while the other team recieves nothing. A good strategy for Thisis to have two points and spawn trap the other team to keep them from earning a lot of points.

Rewards

The main source of rewards in this playlist is the tokens at the end of the game. These tokens can be spent at Saladin to aquire gun and armor rewards. The weekly bounties supplied by Saladin also give the player a pinnacle loot drop from the pool. Some notable weapons include: Swarm of the Raven and Roar of the Bear.
